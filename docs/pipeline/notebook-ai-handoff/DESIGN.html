3 The honest boundary

🔒
The app never signs into ChatGPT. There is no official way for any app to drive your ChatGPT account, your custom GPT, or your Plus plan. The entire "connection" is your clipboard + a link — the app prepares the prompt; you carry it across; ChatGPT does the work on your account. The summary therefore lives in ChatGPT, not back in the app.

If you'd rather the summary appear inside the app, that's a separate path — paste an OpenAI API key once and the app summarizes itself. It's a replica of your GPT, billed to a separate API account (not your ChatGPT Plus). That path is on the backlog; this document covers the shipped hand-off.
